# Finances Interview

## Opening Interview Policy

- Ask one question at a time.
- Opening set should be high-level and capped at 6 questions.
- Require approval before each write.
- Convert relative dates to explicit dates before final save.

## Seed Questions (Fallback)
1. What matters most in finances over the next 90 days?
2. What is working well today, and what is not?
3. Which constraints are blocking progress?
4. What would make the next 30 days successful?
